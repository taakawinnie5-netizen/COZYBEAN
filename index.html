About Us

The heart between every cup

Welcome to Cozy Bean

Born in the heart of Kampala in 2024, Cozy Bean started w ith a simple belief: coffee should be more than caffeine.It should be a moment of comfort, a pause in your busy day, a reason to connect.

Our Promise

We source premium Arabica beans directly from Ugandan farmers in the Rwenzori and Elogon regions.Each batch is small-roasted to bring out rich chocolate and caramel notes you won't find anywher else.

More Than Coffee

Whether your meeting friends,grabing a quick meal or needing a quite corner to work,we built cozy as your second home. Free WIFI,warm smilies and consistently great coffee - that is our gratitude.
